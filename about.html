About Me

Isn't it fascinating how technology and psychology can come together to truly support people? I create projects that offer tools, experiences, and resources designed to nurture mental health and wellbeing, blending insight with innovation.


The human mind is endlessly captivating, how we feel, think, and cope, and I love exploring this through technology. Each project I build is a bridge between understanding and action, helping people reflect, grow, and feel supported in meaningful ways.


My approach combines empathy, research, and creativity to make tools that are not only functional but thoughtful. I am constantly learning and experimenting because in this space where mind meets machine, there is always a new way to make a positive impact.
